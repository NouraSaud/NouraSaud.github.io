MARKET ANALYSIS

With the recent Saudi Arabia’s 2030 vision program, the telecom sector’s are focusing in technologies such as cloud computing and encouraging ICT development feature heavily in Saudi Arabia’s vision for the future. Also, the mobile sector has become an important component of Saudi Arabia’s telecom industry and contributes around 75% to overall telecoms revenues.

RECENT TRENDS

The majority of the population in Saudi Arabia is under the age of 18 which is clearly shaping the market trends as they relate to the telecom sector there is a lot of interest in social media and because of that reason there is a lot of uptake of data to the extent that Saudi Arabia now ranks number one in the world in the use of YouTube. These trends and uses of social media shape the way that telecom companies are reacting to consumers.

MANAGEMENT PLANNING

Project management of a typical telecommunications system or a network would involve managing numerous tasks successfully.
Project management seeks to:

  • Define project scope: goals and objectives
  • Identify and specify tasks/sub-tasks
  • Estimate resource requirements
  • Identify and allocate resources
  • Prepare and execute a project plan
  • Manage critical paths on the project plan
  • Ensure project completion on time and under budget
